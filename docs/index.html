See It in Action

Market research with memory and discipline.

Trade-Ops gives the agent source access, local state, and rules it must respect. The result is not certainty. It is a research process that becomes more honest, reviewable, and repeatable each time it runs.

Features

Built for an AI-native trading desk.

Lives Around TradingView

TradingView remains the cockpit. Trade-Ops adds agent-readable account checks, chart context, paper-trading discipline, and journal state around it.

Grows the Longer You Use It

Journal records and wiki memory turn reviews into reusable context. The next agent session starts with more process knowledge than the last one.

Surveys Fragmented Markets

Equities, crypto, prediction markets, macro, filings, DEX pools, on-chain context, and news sources are exposed as small composable tools.

Creates Signal Candidates

Raw facts become observations, signals, candidates, memos, risk checks, and only then paper trade plans with thesis, entry, stop, and target. You stay in control of what gets traded.

Keeps State Honest

Active account state comes from TradingView, live research stays local, and public examples are kept separate from personal trading records.

Challenges Its Own Thesis

Skeptic review, counter-evidence, stale-data checks, invalidation rules, and portfolio risk limits keep the agent from simply agreeing with momentum.
