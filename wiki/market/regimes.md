## Regime Checklist

Before sizing a new position, assess:

- [ ] VIX above 20? → reduce size by 25–50%
- [ ] Fear & Greed below 25? → crypto setups are contrarian — wait for stabilization or size down
- [ ] Yield curve inverted? → watch for credit stress signals
- [ ] Dollar strengthening sharply? → headwind for commodities and crypto
- [ ] Earnings season active? → check earnings dates before entering equities
