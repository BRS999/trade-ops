# Setup — Pullback

**Last updated:** 2026-04-09

---

## Definition

Entry on a retest of a prior breakout level, moving average, or support zone after an impulsive move. Thesis is trend continuation after the retest holds.

---

## My Conditions (required before entry)

- Prior impulsive move establishes the trend direction
- Price retraces to a logical support level (prior breakout, 21EMA, weekly level)
- Retest shows signs of holding — reduced selling volume, tight candles, or reclaim
- Broader market / base asset (BTC for crypto) is constructive

## My Edge

Best sub-type: **weekly breakout retest** on crypto.
- Asset breaks out of a multi-week range on weekly timeframe
- Pulls back to retest the breakout level
- BTC holding key support is a required condition

## Common Mistakes

- Entering too early on the retest — wait for signs of hold, not just price at level
- Ignoring base asset (BTC) — crypto pullbacks fail if BTC is rolling over
