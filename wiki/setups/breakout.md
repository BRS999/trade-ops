# Setup — Breakout

**Last updated:** 2026-04-09

---

## Definition

Entry above a key resistance level (prior high, range top, pivot) on expanding volume. Thesis is continuation in the direction of the break.

---

## My Conditions (required before entry)

- Price consolidates near resistance for at least 3–5 bars — tight range, volume dry-up
- Volume expands on the break bar
- Broader market (QQQ/BTC) is constructive — no counter-trend environment
- Clear invalidation level below the consolidation low

## My Edge

Best sub-type: **post-earnings tight consolidation breakout** on equities.
- Stock gaps up on earnings, consolidates in a tight range above the 21EMA
- Volume dries up during consolidation
- Breakout above range high triggers entry

## Common Mistakes

- Entering before the break (anticipatory entry) — wait for the candle close above
- Chasing extended breakouts — if it's 3%+ above the trigger, skip it
- Ignoring broader market context
